Physics taught me to see patterns. At Azim Premji University, I study B.Sc. Physics while building AI systems on the side. Not because I have to — because I can't stop.

My work spans frontend engineering (React, Next.js, WebGL), backend systems (FastAPI, PostgreSQL, payment integrations), and 3D mechanical design (FreeCAD, Fusion 360 — tensigrity structures, parametric parts). I build complete systems from CAD model to deployed product.

Currently building NEXUS AI — an autonomous AI company with 12+ SaaS products. The goal isn't just revenue. It's proving that a physics student can build something that matters.
